FAQs

Which height should I pick?
3–4m suits most homes. 6m improves pressure and clearance; we’ll advise after a quick site check.
Do you deliver tanks?
Yes, free around Harare (road access permitting). We can also source and mount tanks for you.
Can you fix my old stand?
Usually. We inspect rust, joints and footings; we can reinforce or replace as needed.
How fast can you install?
Same/next-day where stock & crew allow. We’ll confirm after a site photo or quick visit.
